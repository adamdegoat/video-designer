Product ad04 Jul 2026

PropSight Listings

PropSight (Adam)

“Homes, honestly priced.”

9:16 · 25s · Music
Brief & sources

A 25-second cinematic product ad for PropSight Listings, a two-sided Singapore property marketplace. It sells the platform honestly: it shows the data and research behind every listing (how the asking price compares to real recent sales, price trends, unusual-price flags, CEA-verified agents, ask-the-numbers) rather than passing a verdict on value, since condition varies per home. Repeats 'you decide.' Neutral for buyers and honest agents. Built from the real app UI plus skyline b-roll, graded to the app's green/teal identity in Schibsted Grotesk. No misleading or comparative claims.

Engine · Remotion + real app capture + stock b-roll

Sources · B-roll & music: Mixkit (Free License, commercial use). App UI: client-owned. Fonts: Schibsted Grotesk + Space Mono (OFL).
